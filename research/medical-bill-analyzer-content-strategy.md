# Medical Bill Analyzer — Content Strategy & Video Ideas

*Saved: 2026-05-11*

## Core Thesis

Faceless video works extremely well in the finance/life hack niche. The screen recording format is the strongest play:
- No face needed ever
- The product demos itself naturally
- The "reveal" moment (you were overcharged 10x) is inherently dramatic and shareable
- Works across TikTok, Reels, Shorts

Dollar For precedent: single 60-second faceless educational video about 501(r) charity care erased $55M+ in debt. Same format, same topic area.

---

## Video Formats

### 1. Screen Recording + Voiceover (Primary)
Upload bill → AI analyzes → reveal the overcharge. The moment it says "you were billed $8,400, Medicare rate is $890" IS the content. Just your voice over it. Inherently dramatic, no face needed.

### 2. Text Card Slideshow + Voiceover (Secondary)
Pure information delivery. ElevenLabs TTS for voice if you don't want to record yourself. Generate via larry-marketing skill.

### 3. "I Analyzed 100 Medical Bills" Data Series
Find egregious examples (real or realistic demo bills), show the breakdown. Outrage + education = shares. Can do periodically as the tool gets real usage data.

## Hook Scripts

### Outrage Hooks (highest share potential)
1. "The hospital charged you 10x what Medicare pays. Here's proof."
2. "Your $8,000 bill might be illegal — here's how to check"
3. "I ran a $24,000 hospital bill through AI. The result was insane."
4. "Hospitals are charging random prices and hoping you just pay"
5. "This hospital billed $1,200 for a single Tylenol. No seriously."

### Information Hooks (education → trust → conversion)
6. "501(r) law means hospitals have to forgive your debt. Most people don't know it exists."
7. "If you make under $X/year, your hospital bill might be $0. Here's how to find out."
8. "There's a federal database with what hospitals are actually allowed to charge. No one talks about it."
9. "Medical billing errors affect 80% of hospital bills. Here's how to spot them."
10. "The difference between what your hospital charges and what Medicare pays is called the 'chargemaster.' It's wild."

### Before/After Hooks (conversion)
11. "Bill before: $12,400. Bill after using this free tool: $2,100. Here's what changed."
12. "She was about to pay $9,000. Then she uploaded her bill."
13. "Took 2 minutes. Found $4,200 in billing errors."

### Series Hooks
14. "Real hospital bill #47: Let's see what they tried to pull"
15. "I analyze a real medical bill every week. This one was crazy."

## Posting Strategy

- **Cadence:** 2-3x/day across TikTok, Reels, Shorts (same content, repurposed)
- **Primary platform to prioritize:** TikTok (highest organic reach for this niche)
- **AEO play:** Post in r/HospitalBills, r/MedicalBill, r/povertyfinance with the tool as the solution
- **Best performing format to lead with:** Screen recording of the tool analyzing a demo bill

---

## Production Setup (Student Budget)

- **Screen recording:** QuickTime (free, built into Mac) or OBS
- **Voiceover:** Record your own voice (most authentic) or ElevenLabs TTS
- **Slideshows:** larry-marketing skill (automated AI image + text overlay generation)
- **No face required:** Screen + voiceover is the full stack

---

## Why This Works

1. People are already searching for help with medical bills — massive existing demand
2. The tool does something real (finds actual overcharges) so the content isn't fake
3. Outrage + actionable solution = share-worthy formula
4. No comparable viral presence in this space yet (Dollar For covers charity care only)
5. Every video is a product demo that builds trust simultaneously
